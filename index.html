Salmon Saviors: a multiplayer social impact game

Salmon Saviors is an immersive game designed to simulate the challenges salmon face during their journey back to their spawning grounds.

Up to three players use different peripherals to help salmon to their spawn:

  • The scrub brush removes water pollution that harms the fish. It can also be used to clean pollution off the fish, preventing loss of salmon.
  • The hammer is used to build fish ladders, guiding fish over dams that obstruct their migration. hit the hammer where you want to place the fish ladder.
  • The salmon controller uses the joystick to move a school of fish across the screen. LED lights on the controller indicate how many salmon are still alive.
    • Please see the Design Process PDF below for more pictures!

    The game aims to educate players while promoting environmental stewardship and conservation efforts for salmon habitats.
